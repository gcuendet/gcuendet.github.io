About me

Welcome! I am a PhD student and research assistant in the Signal Processing Lab (LTS5) at Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL).

My main research interests include Computer Vision and Machine Learning and more specifically Facial Images Analysis. I am focusing on applications of automatic Face modelling for medical diagnosis.

Research topic: Prediction of difficult intubation

Among those applications I work on the prediction of difficult endotracheal intubation, a pre-operative assessment problem in anaesthesiology. The assessment is based on images and videos of the face of real patients. Fitting a face model on these images allows us to automatically extract specific morphological features of the patient. Using machine learning techniques, those features are used to provide the final assessment.
